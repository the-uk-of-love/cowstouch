Visit Us: Hare Krishna Temple — Watford

This is our base, where we practise and share the care and protection of cows. You are welcome to visit, learn more, and obtain authentic materials from our cows.

What We Currently Provide
At present, we can provide raw cow dung and raw cow urine only — offered in their natural, unprocessed form, as collected directly from well-cared-for cows. We do not yet offer purified or processed versions, nor ghee or milk, though these may become available in future.

Please note: These natural materials are provided for traditional, agricultural, or spiritual uses only. They are not intended as medical products or substitutes for professional healthcare.

Finding Other Suitable Farms & Partners

If you are not local to Watford, you can connect with responsible cow-protecting farms and communities elsewhere.

Look for places that:

  • ✦ Keep cows, bulls, and older animals for their full natural lifespan
  • ✦ Respect their well-being and do not dispose of them when milk production drops
  • ✦ Use and share their full natural gifts: milk, ghee, dung, and urine

Where to look:

Local Hare Krishna centres and spiritual communities

Ethical and organic farming networks

Community-supported agriculture groups

Traditional and sustainable farming associations

If you know of a farm that fits this vision and would like it mentioned here, feel free to get in touch.
